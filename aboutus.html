About MoonBean Café

More than just coffee, we're a cozy space where moments happen and connections are made.

Our Story

MoonBean Café was founded in 2024 with a simple idea – to create a cozy and welcoming café where everyone can enjoy quality drinks and good company.

We designed our space especially for students and coffee lovers who need a comfortable place to relax, study, or work.

Today, we stay true to our original promise: good coffee, good vibes, and a place you'll love to come back to.

Mission, Vision & Values
